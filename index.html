For You

This is not something grand.

It is something honest.

✦ ✦ ✦

A place where love exists without pressure, without demand.

some things grow best when left free
♥

I am learning that love does not mean holding tightly.

It means standing close without taking space.

✿ ✿ ✿

Your silence is not distance.

It is simply you being you.

♥

I don’t need to know every version of you to respect you.

❀ ❀ ❀

Whatever you choose to share, I will receive gently.

Whatever you keep, I will not question.

♥

This was meant to be a journal.

A journey of pages.

✦ ✦ ✦

But every time I try to write you, you become more than words.

♥

Not in this life.

Not in any life.

✿ ✿ ✿

I could never finish this book.

Because you are not meant to be completed.

♥

So this is where the pages stop.

Not because the love ends.

❀ ❀ ❀

But because some things are meant to keep growing outside the book.

This love asks for nothing.
It simply stays.
