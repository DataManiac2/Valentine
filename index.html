For You

This was never meant to be perfect.
It was only meant to be honest.

Some things start quietly.

I don’t always know how to match what you feel or when you feel it.

But I notice you. Always.

I am learning that love doesn’t mean being loud or constant.

Sometimes it just means staying.

You once said time matters. That knowing someone takes time.

I believed you. I still do.

There are pages I haven’t written yet. Not because I don’t feel enough.

But because you are not something that can be finished.

If I had all the lives in the world, I still wouldn’t be able to complete this book.

Because you are too vast, too real, too special.

This love does not end here. Or anywhere.
