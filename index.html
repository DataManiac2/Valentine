For you

I didn’t plan this. I just started writing and somehow you were already here.

turn

I don’t know how to explain what you feel like to me. Some days it’s calm. Some days confusing. Some days very quiet.

turn

I’ve learned something though. Love doesn’t always look like effort. Sometimes it looks like restraint.

turn

So if I’ve been quieter lately, it isn’t absence. It’s me learning how to stay without holding too tight.

turn

You once said love is only a part of life. I think you were right. But you are the part that makes the rest make sense.

turn

Some pages don’t get written. Not because they matter less, but because they matter too much.

turn

I could keep writing. In this life. In another one. In ways that still wouldn’t finish you.

turn

So I’ll stop here. Not because this ends, but because it doesn’t.
