For You

This is not a grand gesture.
It is a quiet one.

✦ ✦ ✦

Something that exists without asking for anything in return.

some things begin before we name them

I don’t always understand your silences.

But I have never ignored them.

✦ ✦ ✦

I am learning that love is not constant presence, but steady respect.

You once said that knowing someone takes time.

I didn’t argue.

✦ ✦ ✦

Because rushing has never created anything worth keeping.

There are versions of you I may never fully see.

That does not scare me.

✦ ✦ ✦

Love does not require possession to be real.

This was meant to be a journal.

A journey.

✦ ✦ ✦

But every time I try to complete it, you grow beyond the page.

Not in this life.

Not in any life.

✦ ✦ ✦

I could never finish writing you.

And that is how I know this love does not end.

This book stops here.
What it holds does not.
