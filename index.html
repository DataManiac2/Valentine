This Valentine,

I’m not here to ask for anything.

I love you quietly.
Even when you’re distant.
Even when you’re unsure.

Sometimes you asked me for space.
I listened — not because my love changed,
but because I didn’t want it to feel heavy.

Even if you don’t always like me,
my feelings don’t turn into pressure.

This isn’t sacrifice.
It’s just how I love.

You don’t owe me anything
for being loved.

Happy Valentine’s Day.
